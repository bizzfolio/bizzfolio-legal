## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Bizzfolio LLC ("Bizzfolio," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and protects your information when you use our website at https://bizzfolio.com and our SaaS platform (collectively, the "Service").

Bizzfolio is a SaaS platform that helps freelancers and agencies manage clients, projects, and invoicing.

By using the Service, you agree to the collection and use of information as described in this Privacy Policy.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Account Information
- Name
- Email address
- Password (hashed, never stored in plaintext)
- Business name and details
- Profile information you choose to provide

### 2.2 Business Data
- Client information (names, contact details, notes)
- Project details (descriptions, timelines, status)
- Invoices and billing records
- Time tracking data
- Documents and files you upload

### 2.3 Payment Information
- Payment processing is handled by **Stripe**. We do not store your full credit card number, CVV, or bank account details on our servers.
- We receive and store: last four digits of your card, card brand, billing address, and transaction history.

### 2.4 Authentication Data
- If you sign in via **Google OAuth**, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture from Google. We do not receive or store your Google password.

### 2.5 Automatically Collected Information
- IP address
- Browser type and version
- Device information
- Pages visited and actions taken within the Service
- Cookies and similar technologies (see Section 7)

### 2.6 Information from Third Parties
- Google (via OAuth sign-in)
- Stripe (payment confirmation and subscription status)

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

- Provide, operate, and maintain the Service
- Process payments and manage subscriptions
- Authenticate your identity
- Send transactional emails (account confirmation, password resets, invoices)
- Send service-related notifications
- Respond to support requests
- Improve the Service and develop new features
- Detect, prevent, and address fraud or security issues
- Comply with legal obligations

We do **not** sell your personal information to third parties.

## 4. How We Share Your Information

We may share your information with:

### 4.1 Service Providers
- **Stripe** — Payment processing
- **Cloudflare** — CDN, DNS, security, and hosting infrastructure
- **Google** — OAuth authentication

### 4.2 Legal Requirements
We may disclose your information if required by law, legal process, government request, or to protect the rights, safety, or property of Bizzfolio, our users, or the public.

### 4.3 Business Transfers
In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you of any such change.

## 5. Data Storage and Security

- Your data is stored on infrastructure provided by **Cloudflare** (including Cloudflare D1 database).
- We use encryption in transit (TLS/HTTPS) and at rest where supported by our infrastructure providers.
- Access to production systems is restricted to authorized personnel.
- We implement industry-standard security practices, but no system is 100% secure.

## 6. Data Retention

- We retain your account and business data for as long as your account is active.
- Upon account deletion, we will delete or anonymize your data within 30 days, except where retention is required by law (e.g., financial records, tax obligations).
- Payment transaction records may be retained as required by financial regulations.

## 7. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies for:

- **Authentication** — Session cookies to keep you logged in
- **Preferences** — Remembering your settings
- **Analytics** — Understanding how the Service is used (aggregated, non-personally-identifiable)

We do **not** use third-party advertising trackers.

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect the functionality of the Service.

### 8.2 EEA/UK Residents (GDPR)
If you are in the European Economic Area or United Kingdom, our legal bases for processing include: contract performance, legitimate interests, and consent. You may contact us to exercise your rights under GDPR.
